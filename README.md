# Video to GIF & ASCII Converter

A web-based tool for converting MP4 and MOV videos into GIF and ASCII animations directly in the browser. 

## Features
* **Video to GIF:** Easily convert up to 50MB of video into a GIF file.
* **ASCII Animation:** Generates a real-time ASCII art version of your video.
* **Client-Side Processing:** All conversions happen locally in your browser ensuring complete privacy.

## How to Use
1. Visit the live site: https://bariacg.github.io/video-to-gif/
2. Upload your MP4 or MOV file.
3. Click "Start Conversion" and wait for the process to complete.
4. Download your generated GIF or enjoy the ASCII playback.

## Technologies Used
* HTML, CSS, JavaScript
* [FFmpeg.wasm](https://github.com/ffmpegwasm/ffmpeg.wasm) - For in-browser video processing.
